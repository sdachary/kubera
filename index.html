What Is Kubera?

Self-hosted personal finance OS that takes you from debt → zero → wealth in a defined timeline.

Negative

In debt. Track loans, EMIs, and payoff strategies.

Zero

Debt-free. Foundation building with SIPs.

Positive

Wealthy. Passive income and portfolio growth.

"Clear your liabilities before building wealth." — The one rule.

The Journey

1

Debt Freedom

List loans & EMIs. Avalanche or snowball. Track progress and debt-free date.

2

Foundation

Once debt is healthy, SIP suggestions activate. Even ₹500/month works.

3

Income Target

Set goal: "₹25,000/month by 2030." Reverse-engineer the path.

Why Kubera?

  • Philosophy-first — "Debt first, then wealth" is the foundation
  • Your data stays yours — self-hosted, no surveillance
  • Free AI from day one — no $20/month fees
  • Built for India — NSE/BSE, EMIs, SIPs, ₹ currency
  • Community-driven — features serve users, not shareholders

Key differentiators vs other finance apps:

  • Self-hosted (your data, no subscription)
  • Debt-first philosophy
  • Indian markets (NSE/BSE)
  • Free AI options (OpenRouter/Ollama)
  • Open source (AGPL-3.0)
