Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data You Own

Kubera is self-hosted software. All financial data you enter stays on your own infrastructure. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or financial data on third-party servers.

What Information Kubera Processes

Kubera processes the data you explicitly provide: account balances, transaction details, debt information, investment portfolios, and financial goals. This data is stored exclusively in your own PostgreSQL database on hardware you control.

AI Provider Data

If you choose to connect an AI provider (OpenRouter, Ollama, OpenAI, etc.), prompts and context you send may be processed by that provider according to their privacy policy. This is optional — Kubera works fully without AI features. You control which provider, if any, you connect.

No Tracking

Kubera contains no analytics, telemetry, or tracking code. There are no cookies, no fingerprinting, no third-party scripts. The landing page is served as a static HTML file with no external requests beyond optional font loading from Google Fonts.

Data Security

Communication between your browser and Kubera is encrypted via HTTPS when configured. Database credentials are stored in environment variables on your host. Kubera uses bcrypt/argon2 for password hashing and follows Rails security best practices.

Your Rights

Because you control the infrastructure:

  • You can delete any or all data at any time
  • You can export your data via the application or directly from PostgreSQL
  • You can destroy your entire instance and all data permanently
  • No third party has access to your data without your explicit configuration

Changes

This policy may be updated as Kubera evolves. Since you control the source code, you can review changes before deploying updates. Material changes will be noted in release notes.

Contact

Open an issue at github.com/sdachary/kubera/issues with any questions about this policy.
