Powerful Features

Everything you need to manage your personal finances with complete control and privacy

Privacy First

Self-hosted architecture means your financial data never leaves your server. Complete zero-knowledge guarantee.

Advanced Analytics

Deep insights into your spending patterns with interactive charts, trends, and predictive analytics.

Multi-Account Support

Track multiple bank accounts, credit cards, and investment accounts in one unified dashboard.

Smart Budgeting

Create and track budgets with automatic categorization and real-time alerts when you're overspending.

AI Assistant

Optional AI-powered insights to help you make better financial decisions. Integrate your favorite AI provider.

Open Source

Built on open source technologies. Audit the code, contribute improvements, and customize to your needs.

Kubera Exclusive Features

Built for the debt→zero→wealth journey. Phase 1: Debt Freedom. Phase 2: Foundation Building. Phase 3: Income Target.

Debt Payoff Tracker

Track loans, EMIs, and visualize avalanche/snowball payoff strategies. See exactly when you'll be debt-free.

Dividend SIP Planner

AI suggests stocks based on your income target. Build a dividend portfolio that pays you monthly.

Portfolio Rebalancing

Monthly check-ins with on/off track status. Stay aligned with your target asset allocation automatically.

Recurring Expense Reminders

Never miss an EMI or subscription payment. Smart reminders keep you on track, always.

Free AI

Works with free models via OpenRouter, or fully local via Ollama. No API costs, your data stays yours.

NSE/BSE Support

Built with Indian markets in mind. Track NSE/BSE stocks, mutual funds, and INR-based portfolios.
