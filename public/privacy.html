Privacy Policy

Kubera is built around a simple idea: your financial data belongs to you — always.

Last updated: May 2026

Short version: Kubera is self-hosted software. You run it on your own machine. Your data never touches our servers. There is no cloud, no tracking, no data selling, no third-party access. Period.

What Data Kubera Collects

Kubera does not collect any data. The application is designed to be fully self-hosted. When you install Kubera, everything runs on your own computer or server. No data is sent to us, to any third party, or to any external service unless you explicitly configure it (e.g., connecting to an AI provider of your choice).

  • Financial data — all your account balances, transactions, debts, investments, and net worth are stored locally on your machine in your own PostgreSQL database.
  • Personal information — Kubera does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifying information.
  • Usage data — there is no telemetry, no analytics, no crash reporting, no cookies for tracking.

AI Providers

Kubera offers optional AI features using providers you choose and configure. By default, no AI is active. If you enable AI:

  • You choose the provider: Ollama (runs 100% locally, no data leaves your machine), OpenRouter, or any OpenAI-compatible API.
  • Data sent to your chosen AI provider is limited to the financial context you share during a chat session.
  • We recommend using a local model (Ollama) if you want absolute data privacy.

Data Storage

All data is stored in a PostgreSQL database on the machine where you run Kubera. We do not have access to this database. Database backups, security, and retention are entirely your responsibility.

Third-Party Services

Kubera does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or social media services. The only external connections are those you explicitly configure:

  • AI provider (optional) — you choose which API endpoint to use
  • GitHub (optional) — for checking updates to the software
  • Financial data sources (optional) — if you configure external provider sync

Cookies

Kubera does not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising. The application uses a session cookie only when you are logged in, which is technically necessary for the app to function. This cookie contains no personal data and is not used for any purpose other than keeping you signed in during your browser session.

Your Rights

Because your data never reaches us, there is nothing for us to delete, export, or correct. You have full control:

  • Access — your data is in your PostgreSQL database, fully accessible to you
  • Export — you can export your data at any time via the application or directly from the database
  • Delete — stop the server, delete the database, and all data is gone forever
  • Audit — the entire source code is open (AGPL-3.0) for you to inspect

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Since Kubera is self-hosted, you choose whether and when to update your installation.

Questions

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on GitHub. For deeper technical details, refer to the documentation and source code.
